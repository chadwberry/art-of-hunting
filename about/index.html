Chad Berry

I am a Husband, Father, Hunter, Archer, Writer, Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

For the past 25 years my life in the outdoors has unfolded with a video camera capturing the action. From my first hunt at the age of 11, to exciting adventures in the Northwest Territories, British Columbia, Alberta, Alaska and the Western United States the memories from each hunt have been shared through articles, DVD's and the "Berry's Hunting Adventures" T.V. series that aired on both Dish Network and DirectTV from 2002-2005. Continuing this tradition, The ART of HUNTING will share both hunts from the past and present, along with the hunting tactics my family has used for decades to be successful.

Accomplishments: Archery and hunting are my passion. I've hunted in 7 Western states and 3 Canadian provinces, successfully harvesting turkey, coyote, elk, mule deer, whitetail deer, black bear, pronghorn antelope, caribou and moose with archery equipment, including 14 trophy animals that qualify for the Pope and Young record book. By far, hunting and calling elk has been my obsession, taking nine 6x6 bulls with my bow. When not in the woods, I enjoy competing in archery tournaments having won 3 state championships.
