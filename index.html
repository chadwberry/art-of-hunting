The ART of HUNTING

A little bit about me.

My passion for hunting and the outdoors began many years ago. As I started sharing my adventures online, I was fascinated with how easily I could connect with other outdoorsman. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to share the knowledge I had acquired with other outdoor enthusiasts.

The ART of HUNTING is focused on sharing the experience of the hunt with you. Through both articles and videos we'll give you the tips and tactics that my family has used for decades to be successful. Whether you are a beginner or expert, The ART of HUNTING is a must for any outdoorsman.

Good Huntin', Chad Berry
